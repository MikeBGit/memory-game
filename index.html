How to Play

To Win the Game

Flip a card, then flip a second card. If they are a match-- Great! You're still in the game. You have limited attempts to match all the cards face.

Levels of Difficulty

  • Easy - Find 8 pairs
  • Medium - Find 10 pairs
  • Hard - Find 11 pairs
